About

Gokul Durai, founder of DuGoo Capital

I am a civil engineer by profession. Nevertheless, in 2022, after working for six years in the construction field, I made the decision to fully commit to my passion for trading.

I used to trade buying stock options based on recommendations occasionally before 2022, but I was never profitable. That's when I realized that nobody in this field could make profit from other people's recommendations or tips.

Having concluded that option writers are the only ones capable of generating substantial long-term profits, I've been writing options for a year to acquire knowledge and learn how to execute my own trades.

Since then, I've grown into a successful trader who makes money every month on average.
