About Gokul

Gokul Durai, founder and CEO of DuGoo Capital

I have a professional background in civil engineering, but after working in the construction industry for six years, I made the decision to pursue my passion for trading full-time in 2022.

Before 2022, occasionally I used to trade buying options based on suggestions, but I never turned a profit. At that point, I understood that nobody in this industry could profit from other people's recommendations.

Since I've come to the conclusion that option writers are the only ones who can produce significant long-term profits, I've spent one year writing options in order to gain experience and understand how to make my own trades.

I've since developed into a profitable trader who consistently turns a profit each month.
